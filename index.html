Featured Projects

Genome-wide Analysis of NLR Genes in Amaranth

Comprehensive identification, phylogenetic analysis, duplication patterns, synteny mapping, and expression profiling of nucleotide-binding leucine-rich repeat genes in grain amaranth.

NAC and MYB Transcription Factor Family Analysis

Structural classification, evolutionary expansion patterns, and regulatory network analysis of key transcription factor families in plant species.

Deep Learning for Plant Defense-Response Prediction

Development of neural network-based classification models with multi-layer feature engineering for predicting plant defense-response genes.
