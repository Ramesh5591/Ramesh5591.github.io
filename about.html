About Me

I am a PhD Research Scholar dedicated to advancing the frontiers of computational biology and plant genomics through innovative applications of machine learning and deep learning technologies. My work bridges the gap between biological complexity and computational analysis, leveraging data-driven approaches to unlock insights into plant systems.

My research journey is driven by a passion for understanding the molecular mechanisms underlying plant defense and agronomic performance. By integrating genome-wide analyses with predictive modeling, I aim to contribute to sustainable agriculture and crop improvement strategies that address global food security challenges.

Research Philosophy

Integrative Computational Approaches

I believe in the power of interdisciplinary research that combines genomics, bioinformatics, and artificial intelligence. My approach focuses on developing robust computational frameworks that can extract meaningful biological insights from complex high-throughput data, ultimately translating discoveries into practical applications for crop improvement.
